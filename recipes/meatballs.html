Meatballs

Description

This homemade meatball recipe is a classic, and for great reason! For generations, home cooks have relied on this hearty meatball recipe to show some skills the kitchen. All it takes to achieve this meaty main dish is eight basic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 cup Italian-style bread crumbs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • small onion, finely chopped
  • egg

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400°F. Line 13x9-inch pan with foil; spray with cooking spray.
  2. In large bowl, mix all ingredients. Shape mixture into 24 (1 1/2-inch) meatballs. Place 1 inch apart in pan.
  3. Bake uncovered 18 to 22 minutes or until temperature reaches 160°F and no longer pink in center.
